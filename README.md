# Customer and Churn Analysis

Customer Retention & Churn Analysis is all about solving 3 core problems: Who is churning? , Why are they churning?, How can we retain them?

Your goal is to help business teams answer questions such as:

Why are customers leaving the platform?

Which customer segments are most likely to churn?

How long do customers typically stay active?

What actions can improve customer retention? etc.

Analyse:  
 Customers with shorter tenure show significantly higher churn rates, especially those on month-to-month contracts, indicating the importance of early engagement and incentives to convert customers to longer-term contracts.

------Very Important Insights------


Insight 1 — Contract Type Drives Churn

Your chart Churn by Contract will show:

Contract	Churn
Month-to-month	Highest
One-year	Medium
Two-year	Lowest

📊 Interpretation

Customers without long contracts leave more often.
💡 Business recommendation

Offer discounts for yearly plans.


Insight 2 — New Customers Churn the Most

From Retention vs Tenure:

📊 Early tenure customers churn heavily.

💡 Recommendation:

Create a first-3-month onboarding program.

Example:

welcome offers

support calls

loyalty rewards


Insight 3 — Higher Monthly Charges Increase Churn

From your Monthly Charges vs Churn plot.

📊 Customers paying higher charges churn more.

💡 Recommendation:

Introduce:

bundled plans

loyalty discounts

flexible pricing


Insight 4 — Long Tenure Customers Rarely Churn

📊 Customers staying longer tend to remain loyal.

💡 Recommendation:

Build loyalty programs for long-term customers.
